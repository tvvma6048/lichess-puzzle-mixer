## ⚙️ How to use the software

The application interface organizes your training experience into logical steps.

### Importing a Puzzle Database

The software requires a database of puzzles to function. Many chess websites provide puzzle sets as CSV or SQLite files. Download one of these files to your computer.

1.  Open the application.
2.  Navigate to the "Database" menu item in the top bar.
3.  Choose "Load New Database."
4.  Select the puzzle file you downloaded.
5.  Wait for the progress bar to reach completion.

### Creating a Puzzle Mixer

The mixer allows you to build custom training sessions.

1.  Click the "Mixer" tab on the main screen.
2.  Select your preferred themes from the list provided. You can choose options like "Mate in 1," "Endgame Tactics," or "Knight Forks."
3.  Set your operator. Use "AND" to find puzzles that contain all selected themes. Use "OR" to find puzzles that contain at least one of your selected themes.
4.  Input the number of puzzles you want to practice in this session.
5.  Click "Apply Mix" to load your puzzles.

### Solving Puzzles

Once the puzzle set loads, the screen displays the current board state.

*   Click pieces to move them according to standard chess rules.
*   The application tracks your moves against the stored solution.
*   Use the "Hint" button if you get stuck on a difficult tactic.
*   Use the "Next" button to progress to the next puzzle in your mix.

## 📈 Improving your Training

Consistency leads to better results in chess. We suggest these habits for your practice:

*   **Review Mistakes**: The "History" tab keeps a log of your session. Review puzzles you failed to get right.
*   **Vary Themes**: Rotate your themes weekly. Focus on forks one week and endgames the next.
*   **Manage Difficulty**: Keep your puzzle count per session around thirty problems to maintain focus.

## 🛡 Security and Privacy

This application processes data on your machine. We do not track your puzzle progress or send your personal data to remote servers. The software stays isolated from your other files for security.

## 📄 Frequently Asked Questions

**Does the software require a Lichess account?**
No. You do not need a Lichess account to use this offline tool.

**Can I export my progress?**
Yes. Use the "Export" menu to save your session logs as a text file for review in other programs.

**The application runs slowly on my machine. What can I do?**
Close other programs that use significant memory before you open the mixer. Ensure you have the latest Windows updates installed.

**Where do I find more puzzles?**
Search for "Lichess puzzle database" in your preferred search engine. Many community members share large collections of puzzles that work with this software.

**How do I uninstall the software?**
Open your Windows Settings, go to "Apps," find "lichess-puzzle-mixer" in the list, and select "Uninstall." This removes the files from your computer.
